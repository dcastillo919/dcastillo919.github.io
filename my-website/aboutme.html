So...About Me

My life is easily compared to songs that I listen to because they tell a story.

I have been moving from house to house since I was 3 years old. My parents have seperated by then. I would go from having a calm and easily going enviroment to a strict and angry enviroment. I never minded because I understood that no matter what they both loved me equally.

Around 7 years later, my mom had been in a relationship and had my baby brother Leonardo. It wasn't easy because his father left us and we had to handle being on our own. My brother can be a little over whelming because he doesn't listen and struggles to see far away, but I still love him because he is half my blood. He is still growing now and he is very funny when he wants to be.

As I like I was saying, family is complicated, but everyone deals with their stress differently.

Now I"m just dedicated to school and work. I still visit my dad in Anaheim and the family friends we have over there. I also just focus on my brother because he doesn't understand that soon I'm not going to be around as much. All I can say is that from here, I don't predict the future.

I started to work for Ruby's Diner in Anaheim since I was 16 years old, later on I transfered to the one in Redondo Beack. Many were surprised I could handle the restuarant enviroment because it's tiring if you work long shifts and demanding days. I knew I could handle it because my dad is a hard worker and my mom is stubborn. I guess you can say I am my parents daughter.

School

Most of my school work wasn't as demanding as work, but it was still hard. My classes this year just consist with filler periods. I still enjoy the work I do and my hard work gets payed off.

Anyways...

That's my life right there. I hope you learned something about me and my dedication to anything. Hope to see you soon.
